About Zhong Kui

Zhong Kui (钟馗) is a legendary figure in Chinese folklore, often recognized as the "Demon Queller." He is believed to have the power to capture and command ghosts and evil spirits, protecting people from harm.

Cultural Significance

In Chinese tradition, Zhong Kui is regarded as a symbol of justice and protection. His image is often placed on household doors during festivals to ward off evil spirits and bring peace to the family.

Legends

According to legend, Zhong Kui was a brilliant scholar who failed the imperial examination because of his appearance. After his death, he was appointed by the Underworld Emperor to capture demons and protect humanity.
